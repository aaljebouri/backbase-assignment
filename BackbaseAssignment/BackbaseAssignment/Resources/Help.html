How to use the app

1. Adding a location

To add a location to see its weather, click the + button at the top of the home page. On the next page click and hold the map to drop a pin for the location you want to see weather. Giving the app permission to access location data will allow you to easily see your current location and to select it.

2. Removing a location

To remove a location from the cities list, simply swipe left on the location. Then tap the delete button.

3. Viewing location weather

Simply tap on a location from the list to see the weather in that location.
